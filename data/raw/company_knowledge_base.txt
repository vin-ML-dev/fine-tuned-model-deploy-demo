SECTION 1: COMPANY OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a technology company founded in 2015 and
headquartered in Bengaluru, India, with regional offices in Delhi, Pune, and
Singapore. TechNova specializes in cloud software, AI-powered analytics, and
enterprise IT services. The company serves over 800 enterprise clients across
22 countries and employs approximately 1,200 people. TechNova is privately
held, and its founding CEO is Ananya Rao. The CTO is Vikram Mehta and the
Chief People Officer is Sarah D'Souza.

Key facts:
- Founded: 2015
- Headquarters: Bengaluru, India
- Offices: Bengaluru (HQ), Delhi, Pune, Singapore
- Employees: ~1,200
- CEO: Ananya Rao | CTO: Vikram Mehta | CPO: Sarah D'Souza
- Industry: Cloud software, AI analytics, enterprise IT services

SECTION 3: PRODUCTS AND SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. NovaCloud - A managed cloud hosting platform with auto-scaling, 99.95%
   uptime SLA, and pay-as-you-go pricing. Plans start at INR 4,999/month.
2. NovaAnalytics - An AI-powered business intelligence suite with dashboards,
   forecasting, and anomaly detection. Pricing starts at INR 2,499/user/month.
3. NovaChat - An enterprise chatbot platform for customer support automation,
   supporting 15+ languages. Pricing starts at INR 9,999/month.
4. NovaSecure - A cybersecurity monitoring service including threat detection,
   vulnerability scans, and 24/7 SOC support. Custom enterprise pricing.
5. Professional Services - Consulting, cloud migration, custom AI model
   development, and staff augmentation, billed per engagement.

All products include a 14-day free trial. Enterprise clients receive a
dedicated account manager and onboarding support.

SECTION 5: CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- First response time targets: Critical issues - 30 minutes; High - 2 hours;

SECTION 6: REFUND POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Monthly subscriptions: Full refund if cancellation is requested within 7
  days of the billing date. No refund after 7 days; service continues until
  the end of the billing period.
- Annual subscriptions: Pro-rated refund for unused full months if cancelled
  within the first 90 days. After 90 days, no refund, but the plan can be
  downgraded at renewal.
- Free trials: No charges during the 14-day trial; no refund needed.
- Professional services: Refunds are governed by the individual Statement of
  Work (SOW). Advance payments for undelivered milestones are refundable.
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business
  days after approval.
- To request a refund, email billing@technova.example with the invoice number,
  or raise a request in the customer portal under Billing > Refund Request.
- Refunds are not provided for account suspensions caused by violations of the
  Acceptable Use Policy.

SECTION 7: PRIVACY POLICY (SUMMARY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- TechNova collects only the data necessary to provide its services: account
  details, billing information, usage logs, and support interactions.
- Customer data is never sold to third parties.
- Data is shared only with vetted sub-processors (e.g., cloud infrastructure
  and payment providers) under strict data processing agreements.
- Customers may request access, correction, export, or deletion of their
  personal data by emailing privacy@technova.example. Requests are fulfilled
  within 30 days.
- Cookies are used for authentication, preferences, and product analytics.
  Non-essential cookies can be disabled in account settings.
- TechNova complies with India's DPDP Act, GDPR (for EU customers), and
  Singapore's PDPA.
- Data Protection Officer (DPO): dpo@technova.example.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8: DATA RETENTION POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Active customer data is retained for the duration of the subscription.
- After account closure, customer data is retained for 90 days (grace period
  for reactivation), then permanently deleted within 30 additional days.
- Backups are encrypted and retained for 35 days on a rolling basis.
- Billing and tax records are retained for 8 years as required by law.
- Support tickets and chat transcripts are retained for 3 years.
- Application logs are retained for 180 days; security logs for 1 year.
- Customers can request early deletion of their data after account closure,
  subject to legal retention requirements.

SECTION 9: EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (KEY POINTS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Probation period: 6 months for all new hires, with a review at the end.
- Work model: Hybrid. Employees work from office at least 3 days per week
  (Tuesday to Thursday are anchor days). Fully remote roles require VP
  approval.
- Working hours: 9 hours per day including a 1-hour lunch break. Flexible
  start time between 8:00 AM and 10:30 AM.
- Dress code: Smart casual. Formal wear for client meetings.
- Payroll: Salary is credited on the last working day of each month.
- Notice period: 60 days for all employees; 90 days for senior management.
- Employee referral bonus: INR 50,000 for engineering roles, INR 25,000 for
  other roles, paid after the referred employee completes 3 months.
- Health insurance: Company-paid coverage of INR 10 lakh per employee,
  extendable to spouse, children, and parents at subsidized rates.
- IT equipment: All employees receive a company laptop. Damage or loss must
  be reported to IT within 24 hours.

SECTION 10: LEAVE POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Annual paid leave: 24 days per calendar year, accrued monthly (2 per month).
- Sick leave: 12 days per year. A medical certificate is required for more
  than 2 consecutive sick days.
- Casual leave: Included within the 24 annual leave days.
- Public holidays: 10 fixed holidays plus 2 floating holidays of the
  employee's choice.
- Maternity leave: 26 weeks fully paid, as per law.
- Paternity leave: 4 weeks fully paid, to be used within 6 months of the
  child's birth.
- Bereavement leave: 5 days for immediate family.
- Leave carry-forward: Up to 8 unused leave days can be carried to the next
  year; the rest lapse. Leave encashment happens only at exit.
- Leave application: Apply through the HR portal (NovaHR) at least 3 working
  days in advance for planned leave. Manager approval is required.
- Unapproved absence of more than 3 consecutive days is treated as
  absconding and triggers an HR inquiry.

SECTION 12: INFORMATION SECURITY RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all company systems.
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters and changed every 180 days;
  password reuse across systems is prohibited.
- All laptops are encrypted with full-disk encryption and managed by IT (MDM).
- Company data must be stored only in approved systems (Google Workspace,
  NovaCloud internal, approved SaaS). Personal drives and USB storage are
  prohibited for company data.
- Phishing or suspicious emails must be reported via the "Report Phish"
  button or to security@technova.example within 1 hour of noticing.
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT Security immediately (within
  4 hours) so the device can be remotely wiped.
- Access follows the principle of least privilege and is reviewed quarterly.
- Customer production data must never be copied to local machines or used in
  test environments without anonymization.
- Security incidents are handled by the Security Operations Center (SOC);
  employees must not attempt to investigate on their own.
- Annual security awareness training is mandatory for all employees.

SECTION 13: TRAVEL POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- All business travel must be pre-approved by the reporting manager and booked
  through the corporate travel portal (NovaTravel).
- Domestic flights: Economy class. International flights: Economy for flights
  under 6 hours; Premium Economy allowed for longer flights; Business class
  only for VP level and above with CFO approval.
- Hotel caps: Metro cities in India - INR 7,500/night; other Indian cities -
  INR 5,000/night; international - USD 180/night unless pre-approved.
- Daily meal allowance (per diem): INR 1,500 domestic; USD 60 international.
  Receipts are required for reimbursement above per diem.
- Local travel: Ride-hailing (Uber/Ola) or prepaid taxis are reimbursable;
  personal car usage is reimbursed at INR 12/km.
- Expense claims must be submitted in NovaHR within 15 days of trip
  completion, with receipts. Claims after 30 days are not reimbursed.
- Travel insurance is provided by the company for all international trips.
- Combining personal travel with business trips is allowed with prior manager
  approval; personal portions are at the employee's own cost.

SECTION 14: PERFORMANCE REVIEW PROCESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Review cycle: Twice a year - mid-year review (July) and annual review
  (January).
- Process: Self-assessment -> Manager assessment -> Calibration committee ->
  Final rating and feedback conversation.
- Rating scale: 5 levels - Outstanding (5), Exceeds Expectations (4), Meets
  Expectations (3), Needs Improvement (2), Unsatisfactory (1).
- Goals: Every employee sets OKRs at the start of each half, aligned with
  team and company objectives. OKRs are tracked in NovaHR.
- Promotions: Considered during the annual cycle (January). Requires a rating
  of 4 or above and manager nomination.
- Compensation: Annual increments are decided in the January cycle and
  effective from April 1st. Bonus payout is linked to individual rating and
  company performance.
- Performance Improvement Plan (PIP): Employees rated 2 or below may be
  placed on a 60-day PIP with clear goals and weekly check-ins. Failure to
  meet PIP goals may lead to role change or exit.
- 360-degree feedback: Peer feedback is collected for all employees in the
  annual cycle; it is advisory and visible to the manager.
